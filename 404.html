Free online BPM finder for producers, DJs, and dancers

BPMfinder.ca is a free BPM webpage that helps you find the tempo of a song online without installing software. Whether you need an MP3 BPM analyzer, a quick WAV tempo check, or a FLAC beat detector, this tool runs in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

How to use this free tempo detector

  1. Open BPMfinder.ca (bookmark it for quick access).
  2. Drop an audio file or click to browse.
  3. Read the detected BPM and confidence score.
  4. Use Half / Double if the groove is half-time or double-time.
  5. Use Tap tempo to match a beat by hand.

Why use a browser-based BPM tool?

Many paid DAWs include tempo detection, but a free online BPM finder is faster when you are on a phone, laptop, or studio machine without plugins. Because analysis is client-side, it is suitable for unreleased demos and private practice tracks.

Frequently asked questions

Is this BPM finder free?
Yes — 100% free, no account required.
Is my music uploaded?
No. Detection uses your browser only; audio does not leave your device.
What is a good BPM confidence score?
Higher is better. Very short clips or ambient tracks may score lower — try a longer section with clear drums.
Can I use this for DJ mixing?
Yes. Knowing track BPM helps beatmatching and playlist planning.
